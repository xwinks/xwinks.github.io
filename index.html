Wenke Xia (夏文科)

I am a second year PhD student in GeWu-Lab at Gaoling School of Artificial Intelligence, Renmin University of China. I am advised by Prof. Di Hu. Now my research interests focus on generalizable robotic manipulation and emboided AI.

I received my bachelor's degree at the College of Software, Jilin University in 2022. I also awarded the National Scholarship in Jilin University.
